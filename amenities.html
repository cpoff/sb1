SaddleBrooke One residents enjoy private resort style amenities. In addition to our 27 holes of golf, our various amenities provide residents with places to be active, relaxed, and/or social. In addition to the amenities located within SaddleBrooke One, our residents also have use of the SaddleBrooke Two amenities.

Activities Center keyboard_arrow_down

The activity center can accommodate up to 128 people. It serves as a meeting place for larger groups, committees, seminars, and entertainment. With a complete kitchen it is frequently used by clubs and social groups for meetings and pot lucks.

Arts and Crafts Center keyboard_arrow_down

Rooms are available in the Arts and Crafts Center for sewing, quilting, pottery, paper crafting, and other creative arts. There is a gift shop manned by the artisans where they sell their creations. It is a great place to pick up a unique gift for a friend or for yourself.

Bocce Ball Courts keyboard_arrow_down

SaddleBrooke One has four newly resurfaced Bocce Ball courts that are available for open play or they can be reserved for group outings.

Catalina Recreation Center keyboard_arrow_down

The Catalina Recreation Center has undergone a renovation and expansion. The center houses a Billiards Hall with 3 competition tables and a card room.

The Billiards Hall is utilized by 2 different clubs. In addition there is availability to reserve the tables for play by any resident.

The card room can be reserved by any club or resident for private functions.

Clubhouse keyboard_arrow_down

The 33,800 square foot SaddleBrooke One club house is where you will find the Vistas Dining Room, Vermillion Dance Floor, Agave Lounge, Road Runner Grill and Patio, a library, meeting rooms, golf pro shop, and administration offices. The House Committee routinely inspects the club house and makes suggestions for improvements to the décor. They also bring any maintenance issues to management’s attention.

Fitness Center keyboard_arrow_down

The SaddleBrooke One Fitness Center is comprised of:

• Aerobics Classroom with floating hardwood floor

• Equipment Room with cardio and strength training machines in addition to free weight equipment

• Dedicated Stretch Area with stretch barres, web-slide for tubing exercises and elevated platform for those with challenges getting down to and up from the floor

• Two Locker Rooms, each containing three showers, sauna, whirlpool spa and lockers

• Twenty five yard Lap Pool with 4 lanes dedicated to lap swimming only (72 lengths = one mile)

• Two massage treatment rooms located between and accessed through the locker rooms

Library keyboard_arrow_down

Library

The SaddleBrooke One library is one of 3 private libraries within the community. The libraries have a varied selection for the leisure reading enjoyment of the residents. They also have large print books, audio books, videos, and a magazine exchange. The libraries are staffed with volunteers to help you make your selection.

Lounge and Restaurants keyboard_arrow_down

SaddleBrooke One has many dining options. The Road Runner Grill and Patio are open 7 days a week offering breakfast and lunch. Many residents take the opportunity to socialize over a meal before or after their activity. The newly completed Patio is also available for special events.

The Vistas dining room offers up scale dining with panoramic views of the Catalina Mountains. The adjoining Agave Lounge offers more casual dining or a place to relax with friends over a beverage.

There is more detailed information in the Dining Section of this web site. Residents also have the option of dining at the SaddleBrooke HOA2 restaurants. HOA2 offers both casual and fine dining with the Mt. View Bistro and Preserve Grill.

Meeting Rooms keyboard_arrow_down

Numerous rooms of varied sizes are available for resident use. These rooms are used for committee meetings, club meetings, playing cards, etc. They must be reserved in advance due to their popularity.

Pools keyboard_arrow_down

SaddleBrooke One has two swimming pools. A recreational pool with spa and changing rooms that is conveniently located near the Road Runner Grill. While enjoying the pool, residents take in the majestic views of the Catalina Mountains. The pool is kept at a comfortable 87-89 degrees Fahrenheit. There are specified hours that visitors under 18 years of age may use the pool when accompanied by an adult.

The second pool is a lap pool adjacent to the fitness center. The summer water temperature is 80-82 degrees Fahrenheit and the winter water temperature is 82-84 degrees.

SaddleBrooke HOA 2 has an additional 3 pools that may be used under the reciprocal use agreement.

Proshop keyboard_arrow_down

The Golf Proshop has all the needs of the casual or avid golfer. The selection of goods is the best of any golf course pro shop. The goods range from golf tees, gloves, clothing, shoes, hats, to complete club sets and bags. Both of our club professionals are certified in club fitting. More information about Golf and the Proshop is in the Golf Section.

Tennis Center keyboard_arrow_down

The SaddleBrooke One Tennis Center consists of 8 courts, an administration office, and a multiuse covered patio with grill. The courts are used for organized and tournaments play by the Tennis Club. There are also open play times available to all residents.

SaddleBrooke has an active Tennis Club with members from SaddleBrooke One and SaddleBrooke HOA2. SaddleBrooke HOA2 has an additional 13 courts.

The patio is often enjoyed by other groups in need of a meeting place or for socializing.

More information about Tennis is in the Tennis Section.
